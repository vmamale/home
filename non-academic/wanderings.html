Wanderings

Jonkershoek Nature Reserve: Be ready to walk/run more than five kilometers, mountain cylcing, hiking, just sightseeing and other activities can be performed here.

Kruger National Park: Visiting home of animals as visitors is always an mesmerising experience. Here, you can drive your own safari. If you wish to get eaten by a lion, just get out of your car and approach the beast. Other than big five(Lion, Rhino, Leopard, Elephant, Buffalo), Cheetah, Birds are the attractions.

Shivneri Fort: After reading books on Shivaji Maharaj, visiting this place gives goosebumps.

Tiruchendur Murugan Temple: Watching sunrise on the east shore, visiting the temple and coming out to hear ocean again is a beautiful experience.

Brihadeeswara Temple: built in 1010 CE this is one of the UNESCO world heritage sites near Thanjavur. Ganesha idol here, established later by Maratha's(as far as I remember), is really beautiful. Nandi idol is also quite nice.

Airavateswara Temple: This temple has really nice architecture. This is another UNESCO heritage site.

Palaruvi Waterfall: Good trip for half a day in forest.

Shivthar Ghal: surrounded by hills from all sides it's a natural cave with adjacent waterfall. This place was rediscoverd in 1916 after search based on description by Saint Ramdas(1600s). Road trip from Pune via Varandha Ghat road is really good.

Yercaud: Small hill station with paper and coffee plantations. Liked church on a hill, food and newly built temple with wooden idols, nearby.
